About Me

My Travel in Lofoten, Norway

Welcome, and thank you for visiting. This space is both an introduction and an invitation—to explore the paths I’ve walked, the work I pursue, and the interests that continue to shape who I am. I’m delighted to share this journey with you.

Currently

I’m in search of internship adventures where I can blend creativity with code—exploring the worlds of data science, software engineering, and web development, especially in open, English-speaking environments that welcome curiosity and ambition.

These days, I’m immersed in a Master’s program in Data Science, Statistics, and Decision Analysis at Stockholm University, within the Department of Computer and Systems Sciences (DSV). It’s a dynamic mix of logic and imagination—where algorithms meet insight, and where each project becomes a small step toward understanding the world through data.
